News

25 Oct 1999

Er.. TAD was released a couple of weeks ago. If you have bookmarked this page rather than the home page you may have missed it. (thanks Maxwell for the heads up). So hop over to here to get TAD.

30 Sept 1999

Soon! guys, real soon. All maps are now done. Most of the artwork and the coding of the final boss. Your gonna love her!

The only holdup now is getting a quick skin job done on one of the original Quake2 weapon models for the plasma gun due to not having the real thing ready. This sucks but I dont want to wait any longer.

Expect a release over the next few days.....

23 Sept 1999

Things still moving along nicely. Only half of one map to go plus the coding for the final boss and some artwork. The guy who was doing the weapon models has had to duck out so we are currently trying to think of a way to realise the  new weapons that doesnt look too crap. I suspect we will have to do like we did in WOR and just reskin some of the Id models. I know that sucks but I cant delay the release of TAD much longer. If anyone has a good weapon model we can use (view and gun versions) please please please get in touch. I think the release will slip beyond September but hopefully not much.

7 Sept 1999

Hi. We are not dead just busy busy busy. I am still hoping for a TAD release sometime in September but I think it could slip a bit. Patience please......

3 Aug 1999

Things are moving along nicely. Only two maps left and one of those will be a small one. Look for a release toward the end of August or early September

20 July 1999

Well that plea for help fell on deaf ears. No problem. I have decided to carry on regardless. I am still planning to release TAD as soon as possible and in fact we seem to be progressing better now. I think most of the programming is done, over half the maps are done and a major chunk of the artwork. We are waiting on some weapon models but I have every confidence that they will be delivered soon. So watch this space. I assure you it will be worth the wait!

5 July 1999

HELP. I took stock of where we are up to with TAD this weekend. And I realise that if we are to stand a chance of getting out on time we need help. So....

If you are an experienced Quake2 mapper and dont mind working to a strict spec please mail me. I need about 6 maps (architecture only, we will do the entity placement etc) and I need em soon, so you gotta be able to work quick. As this is a non-commercial project, there will of course be no payments, but you will be fully credited when the mod is released. I am hoping to get a few mappers working on 1-2 maps each. We already have half the maps done so I need help from you guys out there to get this kick-ass project done.....
