Enemies

1) Hornet Class Flyer

The hornet is an experimental modification of the flyer. The Stroggs have attempted to bolt a railgun and a shield generator onto a frame which was overstressed already.

In surprise attacks it is deadly especially when hidden within a swarm of standard flyers. But heavy counter attack will cause its shield generator to overload with a small explosion. The resulting smoking and glowing hornet is an easy target.

2) Soldier Storm Trooper

The storm trooper is the elite guard of the Strogg revenge force. Armed with a powerful power screen and a machine gun he is not the cannon fodder you might expect.

3) Laser Gladiator

This is a lethal enemy. Heavily shielded, fast and weilding a feedback laser, this guy is truly terrifying. Beware!

4) Tank General

The top of the tank hierarchy. These guys are slow, but heavily armed and armoured. Trouble especially in groups!

5) Hyper Tank

The end boss. Weilding rockets, grenades blaster and machine gun and with a tendency to use a BFG if things get tough, this guy is a mean boss.
