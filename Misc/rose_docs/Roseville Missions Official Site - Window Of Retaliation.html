Window Of Retaliation

Window Of Retaliation is an nine level mission pack for Quake2.

Set after the defeat of the Makron, you play a pilot involved in a seek and destroy operation, clearing up the last remnants of the Strogg forces.

On a routine mission, you are shot down on an outlying Strogg planet and discover they are planning a revenge attack. You must discover the nature of the attack and stop it before the Earth is destroyed.

Window of Retaliation introduces new weapons and monsters to the standard Quake2 set. Use the links on the left to discover more about this mission.
