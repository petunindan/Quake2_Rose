Weapons

There are three new weapons in addition to all the standard Quake 2 arsenal.

1) Feeback Laser

This is an awesome weapon. It uses cells to charge a high enery laser, but to increase the power, the user can feed the output pulse back through the weapon. This is done continually until the trigger is released when a laser beam stabs out and wreaks havoc on anything in its way.

A visible counter shows the current feedback charge as a percentage value.

A 100% charged Feedback Laser is second only to the BFG in terms of power.

BUT.... Exceed 105% charge and the weapon will overload. Expect to be scattered over a wide area!

This weapon has no blast radius but is highly accurate and fast.

2) Proximity Grenades

These are only available for throwing by hand, they cannot be used with the Grenade Launcher. They work against monsters and other players but will not explode if the user wanders too close. (unless the 1 Minute max delay has expired of course!)

3) Air Force issue Blaster

Pilots are issued with a slightly more powerful version of the standard blaster. This fires two hyperblaster style bolts in quick succession.
